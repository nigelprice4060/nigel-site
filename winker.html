About

The goal of this project was to plan out the layout of my future dorm, now pictured on the right. I wanted to make sure the idea that me and my roomate had in mind was both viable and accessible. I also wanted to see what the lighting looked like before making any purchases.
With that, I had to research all dimensions from my universities website, product pages, and my own belongings. I made the room using hard surface modeling in blender version 3.5, The chair and window blinds were particularly difficult due to the way some components are angled but I was able to do it using a multi resolution modifier and the new slide along edge feature. I then added textures with surface properties and added lighting with the emission feature.
